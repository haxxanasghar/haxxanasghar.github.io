Profile

Hassan Asghar obtained his M.S. in Computer Science from COMSATS University Islamabad, Abbottabad Campus, Pakistan. He received his B.S. in Computer Science from Shaheed Zulfikar Ali Bhutto Institute of Science & Technology (SZABIST), Islamabad, Pakistan. His research interest includes Fault Tolerance in Hadoop, Big Data Analytics, and Cloud Computing.
