Profile

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Chair of Decentralized Systems Engineering at TU Munich (TUM). I received my Ph.D. from the department of Electronic and Information Engineering, Tokyo University of Agriculture and Technology in 2019. My research interests are operating systems, reconfigurable devices, heterogeneous computing, and parallel computing environment. I am a member of ACM, IEEE, and IPSJ.
